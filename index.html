3. Permissions We Request

Depending on your device and enabled features, Avyon POS may request permission to access:

  • Camera — Barcode scanning · QR code scanning · Product image capture
  • Storage — Receipt exports · Reports · Product images · Backup and restore
  • Internet — Cloud synchronization · Authentication · Software updates · Reporting
  • Notifications — Shift reminders · Synchronization status · Important business alerts

These permissions are only used to provide application functionality.

4. Offline Data Storage

Avyon POS is designed to operate offline. Business data may be securely stored on your device while internet access is unavailable. Once connectivity is restored, data may synchronize with our cloud servers.
